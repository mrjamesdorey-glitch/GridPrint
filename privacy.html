GridPrint Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

GridPrint is a Chrome extension that allows users to create printable grids of images, supporting A4 and A3 page sizes with PDF export. All processing is done locally on your device.

Data Collection and Usage:

  • Images are processed locally as data URLs in memory during your session and are not stored persistently.
  • No user data is collected, stored, or transmitted to servers or third parties.
  • No analytics or tracking are used.
